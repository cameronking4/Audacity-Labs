Undergraduate Student

Coach Application


Eligibility

Your are a current sophomore, junior, or senior undergraduate student attending Duke University with a GPA of 3.2 or higher

You are: extremely passion for innovation and entrepreneurship, experienced in leadership roles and team management, have great interpersonal skills and interested in working with teens to develop ideas
As an Audacity Labs Coach, your responsibilities will be the following:
  • Work On-site two days a week for two hours each day
  • Management of a high school team of roughly 6 students
  • Work off-site in management meetings, assisting in research and organization efforts of your team
  • Help students discover opportunities and work with them to identify paths for higher education and post-graduation development
  • Monitor student progress and help flag any potential issues throughout the program
